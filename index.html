How to Measure

1. ERD Effective Rim Diameter

Measure the internal diameter of the rim from nipple bed to nipple bed.

2. Hub Flange Diameter

Measure the diameter of the hub flange where the spokes are attached.

3. Center to Left Flange

Measure from the centerline of the hub to the left flange.

4. Center to Right Flange

Measure from the centerline of the hub to the right flange.

🔘

5. Spoke Hole Diameter

Measure the diameter of the spoke hole in the rim if known.
