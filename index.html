<section class="explanation">

  <div class="card">

    <h2>Formula Explanation</h2>

    <p>The calculator uses a common spoke length formula:</p>

    <div class="formula">

      L = √(R² + r² + d² − 2Rr × cos θ) − spoke hole adjustment

    </div>

    <ul>

      <li><strong>L</strong> = spoke length</li>

      <li><strong>R</strong> = rim radius, calculated as ERD ÷ 2</li>

      <li><strong>r</strong> = hub flange radius, calculated as flange diameter ÷ 2</li>

      <li><strong>d</strong> = center-to-flange distance</li>

      <li><strong>θ</strong> = spoke angle based on spoke count and cross pattern</li>

    </ul>

    <p style="margin-top: 14px;">

      This calculator gives an estimated spoke length. For production or professional wheel building,

      always confirm measurements with the rim and hub manufacturer specifications.

    </p>

  </div>
