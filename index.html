How to Calculate Bicycle Spoke Length

This simple calculator helps wheel builders estimate the correct spoke length for the left and right sides of a bicycle wheel.

Enter the rim ERD, hub flange measurements, spoke count, and cross pattern. The calculator will automatically show the estimated spoke length in millimeters.

Tip: Rear wheels often have different left and right spoke lengths because the hub flange distances are different.

Formula Explanation

The calculator uses a common spoke length formula:

L = √(R² + r² + d² − 2Rr × cos θ) − spoke hole adjustment
  • L = spoke length
  • R = rim radius, calculated as ERD ÷ 2
  • r = hub flange radius, calculated as flange diameter ÷ 2
  • d = center-to-flange distance
  • θ = spoke angle based on spoke count and cross pattern

This calculator gives an estimated spoke length. For production or professional wheel building, always confirm measurements with the rim and hub manufacturer specifications.
